You can load preset in TerraLand's top tab called "Preset Management" and pressing the button "Load Preset" and finally
locating the preset file and hit Open.

You can also save UI settings in TerraLand by going to "Preset Management" and pressing the button "Save Preset" and
finally giving your preset a name and hit Save. This is useful when you want to come back to your saved settings later
or you wish to share settings between firends or own machines.
